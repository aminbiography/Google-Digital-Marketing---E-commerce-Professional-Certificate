# **Planning Job Search With AI**

---

## **A Job Search Plan Must Be Clear and Personalized**

* A strong job search plan **must** outline key steps, skills, and goals.
* AI tools **can** support planning, but all AI output **should** be reviewed and refined.
* The plan **should** stay updated and **may** include transferable skills, identity statements, and timelines.

---

## **Resumes Must Be Tailored for Every Role**

* Each resume **must** be customized to match the job description.
* Measurable achievements **should** be included to strengthen credibility.
* Formatting **should** remain concise, scannable, and professional.
* Errors such as typos **must** be avoided; AI tools **can** assist with proofreading.
* Transferable skills **can** highlight alignment when a career shift **may** be involved.

---

## **AI Support Can Enhance Resume and Cover Letter Quality**

* AI **can** provide structural feedback, sharper phrasing, and stronger summaries.
* Drafts from AI **must** be checked for authenticity, accuracy, and tone.
* Tailoring for each job **should** be done by combining job keywords with personal experience.

---

## **A Cover Letter Should Be Persuasive and Role-Specific**

* A cover letter **should** express motivation, professionalism, and relevant strengths.
* It **must** remain concise, ideally one page.
* It **can** clarify gaps, transitions, or referrals that a resume cannot.
* Relevant achievements **should** be connected to the companyâ€™s needs.

---

## **Employers Will Look for Problem-Solving and Leadership**

* Problem-solving examples **should** show how challenges were approached and resolved.
* Leadership qualities **may** come from work, school, sports, or community involvement.
* These traits **will** help candidates stand out in competitive fields.

---

## **Online Presence Must Be Professional and Consistent**

* A professional online presence **must** be complete, polished, and aligned with career goals.
* Public profiles **should** contain achievements, projects, and industry engagement.
* Risky or unprofessional content **must** be hidden or set to private.
* Platforms such as LinkedIn, Behance, GitHub, and Handshake **can** support different career paths.
* Online activity **may** attract opportunities even when the candidate is offline.

---

## **Persistence Will Be Essential in Any Job Search**

* Rejections **may** happen at different stages.
* Consistent effort **will** increase the chances of securing the right role.
* A strong brand, tailored materials, and active networking **can** make a decisive difference.
