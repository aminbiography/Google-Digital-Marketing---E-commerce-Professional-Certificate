# **AI for Digital Marketing & E-commerce**

#### **1. Understanding AI and Generative AI**

* **AI** performs cognitive tasks like analysis, prediction, and personalization.
* **Generative AI (Gen AI)** creates new content — text, images, or ideas — using prompts.
* Common tools: **Gemini**, **ChatGPT**, and **Microsoft Copilot**.
* Use the **TCREI framework** — *Task, Context, References, Evaluate, Iterate* — to craft effective prompts.

---

#### **2. How AI Supports Digital Marketing**

* **Boosts efficiency** — automates content creation, data analysis, and customer interaction.
* **Enhances creativity** — generates ideas, social media posts, ad copy, and campaign strategies.
* **Improves insights** — analyzes audience data, trends, and sentiment faster than manual research.
* **Personalizes experiences** — tailors messages and ads for specific audience segments.

---

#### **3. Using AI in Key Marketing Areas**

* **Market Research:** Identify target audiences, trends, and unmet needs using gen AI.
* **Campaign Planning:** Brainstorm marketing strategies and messaging ideas.
* **Decision-Making:** Compare campaign concepts with AI’s objective feedback.
* **Email Marketing:** Generate templates, subject lines, and A/B test variations for engagement.
* **Copywriting:** Refine messages, create ad copy, and test different content styles.

---

#### **4. Responsible AI Use**

* Always **verify AI outputs** for accuracy and relevance.
* **Protect privacy:** avoid entering sensitive or confidential information.
* Keep a **human in the loop** to review and refine AI-generated work.
* Disclose AI use when necessary and follow company policies.

---

#### **5. Real-World Application (Justine’s Example)**

* Uses AI to **analyze surveys**, summarize themes, and brainstorm ideas.
* Applies AI to **write and edit content**, saving time and improving clarity.
* Demonstrates a **growth mindset** — from hesitancy to daily integration of AI tools.

---

#### **6. AI in E-commerce**

* Helps forecast **inventory demand**, optimize **ad placement**, and **improve customer retention**.
* Supports building and promoting **e-commerce stores** (e.g., using Shopify, Google Shopping).
* Enables faster creation of **product descriptions, campaign visuals, and reports**.

---

### **Key Takeaways**

* AI is reshaping digital marketing — making processes smarter, faster, and more data-driven.
* Combining **AI efficiency** with **human creativity** leads to the best results.
* Stay curious, keep experimenting, and continue applying what you’ve learned to real-world marketing scenarios.
