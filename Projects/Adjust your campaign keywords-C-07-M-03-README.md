# **Project Title:** **Optimizing Google Ads Keywords for Sinclair Verde Lamp Company**

# **Project Scenario**

Sinclair Verde Lamp Company (Phoenix, AZ) recently launched an online store to build brand awareness and drive visits to its physical location. Their Search campaign (CPC strategy) has underperformed over the past 30 days, with >90% of viewers not clicking. As part of Good Mercury Marketing, I adjusted the campaign’s keywords to maximize clicks while staying within the $1,000/month budget.

# **Project Objective**

* **Primary:** Maximize predicted clicks within a **$1,000** monthly budget.
* **Secondary:** Align keywords with the brand’s **sustainability/upcycling** mission and the target audience (ages **30–55** in the U.S. and Canada who care about décor and eco-friendly living).

# **Project Description:**

**Step 1 — Access & Review Template**
Imported the provided keyword dataset containing: Predicted Impressions, CTR, Clicks, CPC, and Cost.

**Step 2 — Analyze Keywords (Keywords Tab)**
Reviewed all 21 keywords with their predicted metrics to identify high–click-per-dollar terms and mission-aligned intent (eco-friendly, upcycling, waste-to-art).

**Step 3 — Build the Plan (Keyword Planner Tab)**
Selected a set of keywords from the dropdown to **maximize clicks**. Focused on eco-aligned, high-CTR, low-CPC queries.

**Step 4 — Final Keyword Set (Selected Six)**

* **upcycling crafts** — 150 clicks — $112.50
* **environmental-friendly art** — 160 clicks — $96.00
* **environment art** — 90 clicks — $63.00
* **waste crafts** — 225 clicks — $90.00
* **eco-friendly shops** — 300 clicks — $225.00
* **eco-friendly art** — 488 clicks — $487.50

**Totals (Selected Keywords):**

* **Predicted Clicks:** **1,425**
* **Total Cost:** **$986.50**
* **Average CPC:** **≈ $0.69**
* **Remaining Budget:** **$13.50**
  *(Note: Team research suggests updating topics/keywords could lift clicks by ~20%, taking this plan to ~**1,710** clicks.)*

**Step 5 — Budget & Alignment Check**

* Budget ≤ **$1,000** 
* Keywords emphasize **upcycling/eco-friendly** themes that match the company mission and the persona’s interests (sustainability + home décor).
* Supports KPIs: **Lead conversion %, number of new customers**, plus metrics like **link clicks**, **share rate**, and **hashtag reach**.

This keyword optimization delivers **high engagement at low cost**, respects the **$1,000** cap, and tightly aligns with Sinclair Verde’s **sustainable upcycling** brand.
