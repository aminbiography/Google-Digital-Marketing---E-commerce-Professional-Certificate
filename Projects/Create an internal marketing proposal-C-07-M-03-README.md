# **Project Title:** **Internal Marketing Proposal – Sinclair Verde Lamp Company**

---

## **Project Scenario:**

The Sinclair Verde Lamp Company, an eco-conscious furniture retailer based in Phoenix, Arizona, repurposes unwanted materials into unique lamps through an upcycling process. The company recently launched an online store and aims to increase brand awareness and drive both online and in-store traffic.

As an entry-level digital marketer at **Good Mercury Marketing**, your task is to develop an internal marketing proposal focused on increasing brand awareness among adults aged **42–57**—a demographic highly active on social media and motivated by brands that demonstrate environmental responsibility.

---

## **Project Objective:**

To create a data-driven internal marketing proposal that outlines effective strategies and content plans to boost **brand awareness** and highlight Sinclair Verde’s sustainability mission through targeted digital marketing campaigns.

---

## **Project Description – Step by Step:**

### **Step 1: Review Key Client Information**

* Sinclair Verde Lamp Company is dedicated to reducing waste by upcycling unwanted materials into lamps.
* A portion of every sale supports environmental non-profit groups.
* Upcycling conserves vital resources like forests, fuel, and water, while protecting wildlife habitats.
* The company’s mission aligns with environmentally conscious consumers seeking authentic, sustainable brands.

### **Step 2: Analyze Target Audience Statistics**

* **Target Group:** Adults aged **42–57**.
* **95%** use at least one social media platform.
* **Over 50%** engage with brand videos on social media.
* **54%** of Generation X feel overlooked by brands.
* **93%** expect brands to take social or environmental action.
  These insights emphasize the importance of visually engaging, value-driven content distributed through social platforms.

### **Step 3: Select Marketing Strategies / Tactics**

**1. Social Media Advertising**
*Why chosen:*
Social media is the most effective way to reach this demographic, as 95% are active users. Paid ads on **Facebook**, **Instagram**, and **Twitter** can target interests such as home décor, sustainability, and upcycling.
*How it helps:*
These ads will highlight Sinclair Verde’s creative upcycling process, share its environmental contributions, and promote online shopping and in-store visits.

**2. Video Marketing**
*Why chosen:*
Over 50% of the audience engages with brand videos, making video storytelling an ideal way to showcase the brand’s process and values.
*How it helps:*
Short, engaging videos will visually capture the transformation of discarded items into elegant lamps, strengthening emotional connection and inspiring action.

---

### **Step 4: Select Content Bucket and Describe Content**

**Inspirational Content**
*Why chosen:*
This content type aligns perfectly with Sinclair Verde’s mission and the 93% of consumers who expect socially responsible brands. It builds authenticity and emotional resonance.
*Platform and content medium:*
The campaign will use **short videos** and **image-based posts** on **Facebook**, **Instagram**, **Twitter**, and **YouTube**.
*What the audience will see:*
Before-and-after visuals of the upcycling process paired with captions like *“From waste to wonder”* and *“Lighting the way to a greener future.”* Each post will feature Sinclair Verde’s donations to environmental causes and a call-to-action: *“Join us in upcycling the world—one lamp at a time.”*
